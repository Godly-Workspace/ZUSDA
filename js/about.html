Who We Are and What We Stand For

Our Purpose
To nurture followers of Jesus Christ who serve as His devoted ambassadors, sharing the everlasting message of the Three Angels with all people in anticipation of His imminent return.
Discipleship and Evangelism
Our Approach
Led by the Scriptures and the guidance of the Holy Spirit, we fulfill this purpose through Christ-like behavior, communication, mentoring, instruction, care, and service.
Living and Serving Like Christ
Our Aspiration
In alignment with the Bible's prophetic vision, we anticipate the culmination of God’s plan, where all creation is restored to perfect harmony with His divine will and righteousness.
A Vision of Restoration
Our Commitment
We are dedicated to holistic development—fostering spiritual, emotional, and physical well-being. By providing opportunities for worship, education, and outreach.
Holistic Growth and Empowerment
